Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is a recipe for chocolate chip cookies. They are very tasty

Ingrediants

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Mix together dry ingrediants
  2. Mix together wet ingrediants
  3. Mix together until smooth
  4. shape into balls
  5. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes
